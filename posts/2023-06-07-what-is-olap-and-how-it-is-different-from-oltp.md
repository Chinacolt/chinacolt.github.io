MOLAP databases pre-aggregate data into cubes, enabling lightning-fast query responses for complex analytical tasks. This makes MOLAP ideal for scenarios where speed and efficiency are paramount, such as real-time analytics and interactive data exploration.
Last but not least, we have HOLAP, the hybrid OLAP approach that combines the best of both ROLAP and MOLAP worlds. HOLAP strikes a balance between storing some data in multidimensional cubes (like MOLAP) and leveraging relational databases for other data (like ROLAP). It's like having the convenience of a fast-food drive-thru with the quality of a gourmet restaurant – HOLAP offers the flexibility of ROLAP and the performance of MOLAP, making it a versatile choice for diverse analytical needs.
